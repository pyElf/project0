Welcome to the World of Rockclimbing

Substyles

Leadclimbning

Leadclimbing is a discipline of Rockclimbing where the participant climb big walls while securing himself by attaching ropes to the rockwall.

Bouldering

Bouldering is a discipline of Rockclimbing where the participant climb smaller walls with a maximal heigth of 9 feet. The climber places below the rockwall to prevent injuries from falling.

Speed climbing

Speedclimbing is a discepline of Rockclimbing where the participant climb a standard wall as fast as possible.
